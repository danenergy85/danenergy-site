About

Engineer. Cost analyst. Systems thinker.

I'm Danilo Skoric — an Electrical Engineer (MSc, Power Engineering) with a decade of practical work across photovoltaics, industrial automation, automotive production systems and energy infrastructure.

DanEnergy is the consultancy platform behind my work. The mission is simple: combine engineering, economics and energy systems thinking to deliver decisions that hold up under physical and economic scrutiny.

My background spans the full chain — from on-site commissioning of robotics and machine-vision systems for OEM automotive lines, through electrical project coordination on large infrastructure builds, to PV system design and cost engineering for industrial RFQs.

The common thread is rational use of energy and capital. Behind every factory, supply chain and product there is an energy cost; treating it seriously is what separates resilient operations from fragile ones.
